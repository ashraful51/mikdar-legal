Delete Your Mikdar Account

App: Mikdar · Last updated: 19 June 2026

You can permanently delete your Mikdar account and all server-side data we hold about you. There are two ways to do this. The in-app option is fastest and requires no waiting.

Option 1 — In the app (recommended, instant)

  1. Open Mikdar on your phone.
  2. Go to Settings → Backup.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Google Drive section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm the dialog.

This removes your Firestore profile, deletes your Firebase Authentication record, and revokes Mikdar's access to your Google account — all immediately.

Option 2 — By email (if you no longer have the app)

If you have uninstalled Mikdar, lost access to your device, or cannot use the in-app option for any reason, email us and we will delete your account manually.

Email a deletion request

Or copy this email address: info@ezomfy.com

Please include:

  • The email address of the Google account you used to sign in to Mikdar.
  • A short confirmation that you want your account deleted permanently.

We process verified email requests within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed.

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Firestore profile (users/{your-uid}): name, email, photo URL, country, locale, platform, timestamps Deleted permanently.
Firebase Authentication user Deleted permanently.
Mikdar's access to your Google Drive Revoked. The app loses its drive.file grant.
Encrypted backup files in your own Google Drive (Mikdar/ folder) Not deleted automatically. They live in your Drive, not ours, and only you can access them. You can delete them from drive.google.com if you wish.
On-device data (transactions, debts, invoices, reminders, settings, PIN, recovery key) Not affected by account deletion. Use the app's Settings → Reset option, or uninstall the app, to remove on-device data.
Crashlytics crash reports (if you didn't disable them) Anonymous crash reports retained per Google's standard retention policy (typically 90 days). They contain no transaction content.

Want to keep using the app without an account?

You can. Deleting your account does not uninstall Mikdar. After deletion, you remain signed out — every core feature still works locally on your device.

Questions
