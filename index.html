About Mikdar

Mikdar is a free, local-first personal finance app with built-in Islamic finance tools (Zakat, Miras, Waris, Hijri calendar) and a collection of practical calculators (EMI, SIP, currency, unit, time zone, GPA, land, tip, and more). Available in English, বাংলা, and العربية. Works offline by default; cloud features are optional.
